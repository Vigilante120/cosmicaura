Astrology – The Cosmic Science of Life

What is Astrology?

Astrology is an ancient science that studies the movement of planets, stars, and celestial bodies to understand their influence on human life. From the moment you are born, the positions of the planets create a unique birth chart (kundli) that holds the secrets of your personality, strengths, challenges, relationships, and destiny.

Why is Astrology Important?

Life is full of choices and uncertainties. Astrology acts as a cosmic map, showing you the right direction when you feel lost. It helps you understand:

  • Who you are and why you face certain challenges
  • What opportunities will come your way
  • When to make important decisions in career, marriage, business, and education
  • How to overcome difficulties with remedies and guidance

The Power of Planets

The nine planets (Navagrahas)—Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, and Ketu—play a vital role in shaping your life. Each planet controls specific aspects such as health, wealth, relationships, opportunities, and spiritual growth.When they are well-placed, you enjoy progress and happiness. When they are unfavorable, you may face challenges.
